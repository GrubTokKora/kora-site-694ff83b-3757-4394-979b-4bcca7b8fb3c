Welcome to Skin Clinic NY

Skin Clinic NY is a premier dermatology and aesthetic center dedicated to providing personalized skin care solutions. Our team of board-certified dermatologists and skilled aestheticians uses the latest technology and evidence-based practices to help you achieve healthy, beautiful skin. We believe in a holistic approach, combining medical expertise with a passion for aesthetics to deliver results that not only look great but also promote long-term skin health.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services to address all your skin concerns.

Advanced Facials

Customized treatments to cleanse, exfoliate, and nourish your skin for a clear, well-hydrated complexion.

Chemical Peels

Improve skin texture and tone by removing the damaged outer layers, revealing smoother, younger-looking skin.

Microneedling

Stimulate collagen production to reduce fine lines, wrinkles, and acne scars for firmer, rejuvenated skin.

Laser Hair Removal

Achieve long-lasting smooth skin with our safe and effective laser hair removal technology.

Botox & Fillers

Soften wrinkles and restore facial volume with our expertly administered injectable treatments.

CoolSculpting

Non-invasive fat reduction treatment that freezes and eliminates stubborn fat cells permanently.
